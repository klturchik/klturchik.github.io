closeDarkspear Sharpshooter 2 Mana | 1 Attack | 3 Health
"Whenever an enemy minion attacks, deal 1 damage to it."

A simple defense orriented card. It is effectively a 2/3 when attacked by an enemy minion, but it will also deal a small amount of 1 damage whenever an enemy minion attacks any other character. Its "Whenever" text means that it will deal its 1 damage to an enemy BEFORE the enemy deals its damage, so enemy minions with 1 health minions would die without dealing any damage if they attack while this minion is in play.

Darkspear Sharpshooter would probably not see much competitive play on its own, but combined with other similar cards (such as Guard Tower) a new interesting deck could be created. Together, two Darkspear Sharpshooters would synergize with each other by dealing 2 damage to any enemy minion that attacked, and a Darkspear Sharpshooter behind a Guard Tower would deal 3 damage to any enemy minion that attacked.

Darkspear Sharpshooter would also be an interesting card for Arena where simple cards like River Crocolisk are often considered useful. A player might need to change up their strategy if they don't have minions that can deal with with this card.

closeGuard Tower 5 Mana | 0 Attack | 8 Health
"Taunt. Can't Attack. Whenever an enemy minion attacks, deal 2 damage to it."

Similar to Darkspear Sharpshooter, this is a defense orriented card. It has no attack power and has no ability to initiate attacks on its own, but it will protect other friendly characters behind its Taunt and it will deal 2 damage whenever an enemy minion attacks. Its "Whenever" text means that it will deal its 2 damage to an enemy BEFORE the enemy deals its damage, so enemy minions with 2 health would die without dealing any damage if they attack while this minion is in play.

Guard Tower is a high health Taunt, but its 2 damage ability would probably not affect moderate health enemy minions significantly in the mid-game. However, it could be combined with other similar cards (such as Darkspear Sharpshooter) to create an interesting new deck. Together a Guard Tower and Darkspear Sharpshooter would deal 3 damage to any enemy minion that attacked, and 2 Guard Towers would deal 4 damage to any enemy minion that attacked.

Guard Tower would also be an interesting card for Arena where a good balance of Taunt minions is necessary and cards like Fen Creeper are often considered useful. A player might need to change up their strategy if they don't have minions that can deal with with this card.

closeVolatile Frost Axe 3 Mana | 3 Attack | 4 Durability
"After your hero attacks, Freeze your hero."

A very cost efficient weapon with a big downside. Similar to Cursed Blade, this weapon costs about 2 mana less than it normally should. Weapons usually allow you to gain a big boost in tempo by repeatedly removing enemy minions every turn. This weapon only allows you to attack every other turn, making it a better fit in a control deck, than a tempo deck.

I like the idea of a slower weapon for Shamans. Most Shaman decks so far have tilted towards the aggressive side, benefiting from very powerful weapons like Spirit Claws and Doomhammer. Control Shaman decks do exist, but are not very popular right now. I believe an interesting control tool such as this could incentivize players experiment with Control Shaman decks a bit more, without making the archetype too powerful.

The idea for the lore of this weapons came from the Whispers of the Old Gods expansion. I imagined it as a Dark Shaman weapon that has gained its power by enslaving the elements, while at the same time the elements are resisting the will of the wielder.
